jollof Rice

Description

jollof,or jollofrice,is a rice dish from West Africa.The dish is typically made with long-grain rice,tomatoes,onion,spices,vegetables and meat in a single pot,although its ingredient and preparation methods vary across different regions.

ingredient

  • Tomatoes
  • onion
  • pepper
  • meat
  • spices
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Add vegetables oil when it is hot
  2. Make sure it gets fried then add enough water that can cook the rice
  3. Wash your rice and pour it in the boiled water then stir immediately and cook on low heat to avoid it get bunt before it is done
